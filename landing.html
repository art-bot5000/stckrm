How it works

Set it up once. Run it forever.

No spreadsheets. No nagging. Just a quiet, smart system that learns your household rhythm.

01

Add your essentials

Scan a barcode or type a name. Set how often you replace it — that's all the setup you need.

02

Stockroom tracks usage

Mark something as replaced and the timer resets. Days remaining update automatically — across all your devices.

03

Get alerts before you run out

Email reminders highlight what'll need replacing this month — sorted by urgency, with the right shop next to each item.

04

Order everything in one go

One trip. One delivery. One predictable monthly bill. Less waste, less stress, less time on logistics.

One monthly delivery beats eight panic-buys — for your wallet and the planet.

Every time a van runs out to deliver one forgotten item, that's fuel, packaging, and emissions for something you'd have ordered anyway. Stockroom turns the chaos into one well-planned shop. Better for your week. Better for the climate.

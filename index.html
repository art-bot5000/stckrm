About

STOCKROOM — Household Consumables Tracker.
Stock estimates are calculated from your purchase history, quantity per purchase, and months-of-supply settings.
Data syncs securely to the cloud, encrypted on your device. Export regularly as an additional backup.

GROCERY LIST

Import Grocery List

Import a list of items from a file. After importing you'll be able to review, edit names, set departments and add notes before saving.

Supported formats
.txt Plain text — one item per line or separated by commas
.csv Comma-separated values — items in first column, or comma-separated on one line
.tsv Tab-separated — items in first column
.xml XML — text content extracted from all nodes
.md Markdown — bullet points and list items extracted
.rtf Rich text — plain text extracted, formatting stripped
.doc .docx Word documents — text extracted line by line (best saved as .txt first for cleanest results)

Deactivate your account?

Your account will be disabled for up to 3 months. During this time:

  • You will not be able to sign in
  • Your data is preserved on our servers
  • You'll receive a reminder email 1 week before the 3 months is up
  • If no action is taken, a final warning period begins before the account is marked for deletion
Tip: Export your data in the Data section before deactivating.

Delete your account?

This is permanent and cannot be undone.

All your items, notes, groceries, reminders, and household data will be permanently erased from our servers.
